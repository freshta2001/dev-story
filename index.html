My Developer Story

Freshta Mohammadi

When I was a child , I had no idea about computers but my brothers were playing games on their computers all the time and I loved looking at them. When I was 12 years old, I started working with Aria Tv as a children’s program host, everything there was managed by computers and that made me interested in learning computers. At that time I had no chance to learn computers till I graduated from school and I asked my family to buy a laptop for me. My mom bought a laptop for me and she told me to start learning computers because this world is the world of technology and computers. I started playing games, watching movies and listening to music with my first computer for the first time and I was super happy . After that, for some reason, we had to leave our country many times and this made me fail to learn computers. Now I found Refcode and this is the best chance for me to learn computers and I’m happy that I can improve myself to learn coding.
